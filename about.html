About Us

Mount GLory International School

Mount Glory is an English medium co-educational institution established in 2002 and operated under professional academicians. The school has a sole objective of rendering qualitative, contemporary and need and value based education among the students in a congenial environment. Since the school has been operated by academically and socially conscious group, it is completely an organization dedicated for strengthening the institution and overall development of its pupils through advanced teaching approaches and offering a perfect blend of curricular, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities to achieve the overall development.

We believe in holistic education. Thus, we promise to put all possible efforts to impart 'complete education' to the children through advanced technology and methodologies developed and used so far. The school has its own criteria of expenses which directly support its pupils, the teachers and the society at large.

The school located in a peaceful environment in the heart of the city is equipped with adequate facilities. The school aims to provide education based on holistic concept which will equip the children with knowledge, skill, creativity and understandings about the world and themselves. The school management has sincerely pondered for providing balanced, qualitative, scientific, creative and practical education to enable the pupils to meet the challenges of this competitive and fast changing era. For the materialization of its aims and objectives as designed for all round development and upliftment of the students.
